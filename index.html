Mission Statement

Our goal is to bring to light the stigma of tattoos around the world. Today 1 in 5 people have a tattoo yet there is still a negative connotation Associated with them. Permanent Traveler is here to help. We want to send you and your tattoo somewhere to volunteer and help those in need. Stay with us, show the world how great you are, and maybe get a tattoo to remember your journey.
